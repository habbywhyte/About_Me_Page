About Me...

Hi there, my name is Habby and I have no idea how I ended up here (meaning the world of technology) but geesh!...what took me so long? I have always had an affinity for all things creative, but I just didn't know how to apply it until it just hit me!

I'm a creative thinker, and you can usually find me tinkering with colors, images, shapes, fonts, and text placements...etc. I find beauty (and power!) in how they work in concert to inspire an action or a feeling.

I'm a mom of two beautiful, very lively young girls and they are what keep me motivated to achieve all things possible. I want them to see their mom as a strong example of a woman who isn't afraid to take risks and to follow her dream. I want the chance to transform the lives of women and girls everywhere- to become enlightened, inspired and turned on through and by technology.
